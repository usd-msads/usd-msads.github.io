Workshops


OCTOBER 2020

Workshop Overview: a free and guided virtual workshop presented in partnership between the University of San Diego’s Applied Data Science Master’s Program and San Diego Tech Hub. The free 3-part, 90-minute virtual workshop series will develop data science skills, help advance your career, and drive social change. Don’t worry if you are a beginner, there is no requirement for previous programming experience as all the foundational languages (Python, R), skills, and tools (Tableau) will be taught during the workshops. Following completion, you will receive a certificate of completion to be included on your resume.


NOTE: Participants should install Python, R and Tableau on their computers prior to each respective workshop using the instruction materials provided below.
